About me

Hello, I’m a Business & Product Operations Analyst specializing in content operations and analytics, with experience driving data-informed product decisions and scalable workflows across e-commerce platforms.

At KISS Products, I support the B2B platform by building GA4 dashboards, SQL/Excel trackers, and predictive models that uncover buyer trends and guide growth strategies. I partner with developers and QA vendors to validate new features, automate promotion workflows, and streamline product launches.

I hold a B.S. in Information Science, Systems, and Technology (Data Science & UX concentration) from Cornell University, where I developed a strong foundation in data analysis, visualization, and human-centered design. My academic projects range from designing high-fidelity prototypes for smart product interfaces to analyzing NYC’s transportation data for policy and operational insights.

Previously, I gained systems and QA experience as an Infrastructure Development Intern at Wavebridge, working in Agile sprints to refine APIs, validate platform features, and strengthen cross-functional documentation.

I’m motivated by opportunities to bridge data analytics and product management—using insights to enhance customer experiences, optimize operations, and inform business strategy.
